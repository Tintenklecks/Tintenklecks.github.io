Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2024

Introduction

Thank you for using SummarAIzer. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle the data you provide when using our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or user information. All transformations and processes within the app occur in real-time, and no data is retained by SummarAIzer.

Information Shared with Third Parties

When you use the app to process text or URLs, the content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate the requested output. Here are the key details you should know:

  • The content you share (e.g., text or URLs) is transmitted securely to OpenAI's servers for processing.
  • OpenAI does not use this data for training their models or improving their services.
  • The data is processed temporarily and only for the purpose of providing the requested transformation.

Your Responsibility

By using SummarAIzer, you acknowledge that the text or URLs you share are transmitted to OpenAI. Please avoid sharing sensitive or confidential information that you do not wish to be processed by OpenAI's API.

Data Security

We take all reasonable measures to ensure that the transmission of data between your device and OpenAI is secure. This includes using encrypted connections to protect your information during processing.

Your Rights

Since we do not store any personal information, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about data handling by OpenAI, please refer to their privacy policy for additional information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.
