Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

TelePrompt is committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to be simple, straightforward, and respectful of your privacy.

Data Collection

TelePrompt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your prompts and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any external servers.

How Your Data is Stored

All content you create in TelePrompt (including your prompts and timing settings) is stored exclusively on your device. This data is only accessible through your device and the app itself.

Third-Party Services

TelePrompt does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. We don't use any tracking tools or analytics services.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any of your data, there's nothing we need to protect or manage. You have complete control over your content within the app.

Changes to Content

All content management (creation, editing, and deletion) happens locally on your device. You can delete your prompts at any time through the app's interface.

Children's Privacy

TelePrompt is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store page of TelePrompt.
